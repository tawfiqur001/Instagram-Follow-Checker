How to export your Instagram data

1
Go to your profile
Open the Instagram app and tap your profile picture in the bottom-right corner.
2
Open the menu
Tap the ☰ three-line icon in the top-right, then tap Settings and privacy.
3
Go to Accounts Center
Tap Accounts Center, then tap Your information and permissions.
4
Start the export
Tap Export your information, then Create export. Select the Instagram account to export.
5
Set destination & email
Choose Export to device. Under Notify, select the email where Instagram should send the download link.
6
Select Followers & Following only
Under Customize information, open the Connections tab and check only Followers and following.
7
Set date range & format
Set Date range to All time for the whole data. Set Format to JSON. Tap Start export.
8
Confirm with your password
Enter your Instagram password when prompted to authorize the export. The file will be emailed to you shortly.
9
Download the ZIP
Open the email from Instagram, tap the download link, and save the .zip file to your device.
10
Upload here & analyze
Come back to this page, tap the upload area, select your .zip file, then tap Analyze account.
Privacy: Everything runs entirely in your browser. Your data is never uploaded to any server.
